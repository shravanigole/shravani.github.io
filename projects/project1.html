SplitEase - Group Expense Splitting App

Android Application | Java, XML, Room Database | 2025

About the Project

SplitEase is an Android application designed to simplify group expense tracking and bill splitting. Whether you're planning a trip with friends, managing household expenses, or organizing group events, SplitEase helps you keep track of who owes whom, without any awkward conversations.

Key Features

  • Create and manage group expense events (e.g., trips, dinners, shared shopping).
  • Add expenses and assign shares to different participants.
  • Automatically calculates who owes how much to whom.
  • Mark payments as settled and track history.
  • User-friendly interface and clean design for effortless usage.

My Role

I conceptualized, designed, and built the entire app from scratch using Android Studio. I handled both the front-end (using XML) and back-end logic (Java), and implemented persistent local storage using Room Database. The focus was on creating an intuitive UX and ensuring accurate expense calculations.
